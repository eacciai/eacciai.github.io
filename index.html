Welcome to my website!

I am a third-year PhD student in Economics at the University of Turin and Collegio Carlo Alberto, under the supervision of Prof. Marina Della Giusta and Prof. Juan F. Vargas. My research interests lie in Economics of Crime and Conflict, Economics of Culture, and Development Economics. If you want to get in touch, please don't hesitate to contact me!

Beyond research, I'm drawn to art in all its forms — museums, galleries, artist residencies. Traveling is a constant, especially across Asia. Wherever I am, I'm always up for a run.
