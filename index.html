Welcome!

I am an applied micro-economist working in the economics of crime and conflict, cultural economics, development economics, and AI & ML. Two recurring threads in my research: (i) studying cultural heritage preservation and the socioeconomic role of art; (ii) using advances in AI / ML / NLP to extract low-dimensional structure from high-dimensional data.

I am a third-year PhD student in Economics at the University of Turin and Collegio Carlo Alberto, under the supervision of Prof. Marina Della Giusta and Prof. Juan F. Vargas.
